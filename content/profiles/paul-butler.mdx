Paul is a co-founder of Jamsocket, a platform for scaling sync engines. His open-source work includes Plane, a router for scaling WebSocket servers, and Y-Sweet, a Yjs backend that runs on Plane.

Paul also hosts the Local Thirst happy hour series, with past events in NYC, San Francisco, and London.
