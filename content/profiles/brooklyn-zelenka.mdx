Brooklyn is a developer and researcher. She has interests in distributed access control, local-first, E2EE, standards, and open source. She lead the Keyhive team at Ink & Switch, is the spec editor for the UCAN distributed RPC and auth system, and has worked on CRDTs, distributed file systems, databases, and programming languages. Brooklyn is based in beautiful, rainy Vancouver.
