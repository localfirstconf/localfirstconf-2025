---
title: The disappearing framework, and many concepts you don't need with Local First
speaker: nathan-wienert
start: 2025-05-28T15:45+02:00
duration: 15
location: Main Stage
---

Frameworks have a weird relationship with data-fetching, often providing partial solutions to an ostensibly wide problem space. Local-first aims to solve "all" of data - querying, fetching, mutation, storage, caching, realtime sync, and the new generation does it better than any framework. This talk will make the case for why shifting data into a library makes for better UX, and a simpler framework.
